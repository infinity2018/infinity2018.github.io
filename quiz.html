Details

We, the students of department of mathematics and statistics, have been organizing our fest named ‘INFINITY’ since 5 years. Our aim is to grow interest in mathematics, statistics and logical thinking. In this year, 2018, we add a new event ‘Master Mind’, logical event where everyone studying in B.Sc. can participate in the event. Here is an event ‘Colloquium’ where selected participants can represent themselves on stage and face some questions by the audience and professors. The inauguration of the fest will commence with a short lecture of our Maharaj after the prelims at about 10:30 am. The fest will continue till 5pm.
In an educational context, a quiz is usually a form of a student assessment. We have organized a ‘Quiz’ event to check comprehension of a type of mathematical exercise. It will be conducted through two parts prelims (expected time of starting at 9:30 am) and ‘On stage rounds’ (expected time of starting at around 11:30 am). Each team should have two participants. On the basis of prelims six teams will be selected for finals.
The ‘Quiz’ event consists of five rounds. There will be no elimination for first round while in the next round there will be double elimination. At the end of third and fourth round there will be single elimination. At last, the final round will see the emergence of the winner.
Please obey the following rules for participating ‘Quiz’:
  1. Each team must have two participants.
  2. Both the participants are requested to report to the venue by 9:00 am latest.
  3. Both are requested to bring their college id card.
  4. No electronic gadgets are allowed during Quiz prelims.

    5. On spot registration will also be available.
